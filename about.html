About Gcreation

Gcreation is a personal digital invention space founded by Fariz Gavriel Alvaro — a 12-year-old creator from Indonesia. With a passion for web development, innovation, and technology, Fariz builds fast, creative, and minimal digital tools that inspire and teach.

The mission of Gcreation is to Invent. Refine. Share. Every project is made to improve skills, solve small problems, or just create something cool and fun for others to try.
